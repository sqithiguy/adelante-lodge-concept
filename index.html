Welcome to Adelante Lodge

A home-from-home base for Bloemfontein stays

Adelante Lodge is designed for guests who want comfort, convenience and a calm place to rest while staying near Bloemfontein’s business, medical, sport and shopping hubs. Whether you are travelling for work, family, study, treatment, sport or a stopover from the N1, the lodge offers a grounded and dependable place to stay.

The experience is practical without feeling cold: secure parking, a garden and pool area, breakfast availability, 24-hour assistance and room setups that suit solo travellers, colleagues, couples and families.

Explore Bloemfontein

A convenient base for work, family visits and local exploring

Culture and history

Oliewenhuis Art Museum, the Mandela Statue, historical buildings and the National Women’s Monument.

Nature and family outings

Franklin Game Reserve, Naval Hill Planetarium, the National Botanical Gardens and Maselspoort Resort.

Shopping and city convenience

Loch Logan Waterfront, markets, restaurants, family activities and easy city access.
